FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I download The System?

You can find all download links on our Downloads section on the home page. Make sure to check the system requirements before installing.

What are the system requirements for The System (PC)?

The System currently requires Windows 10 (64-bit) or higher, a minimum of 8GB RAM, and 20GB of free storage. A dedicated graphics card is recommended for optimal performance.

Is The System free to use?

Yes, The System is currently available for free for personal use.

Where can I get technical support?

For immediate technical support, the best place to get help is our official Discord server. Our community and support team are active there to assist you. You can also send us an email for non-urgent inquiries.

How often is The System updated?

We regularly release updates for The System to introduce new features, improve performance, and fix bugs. Keep an eye on our "Events" section on the home page or follow our social media channels for announcements!
